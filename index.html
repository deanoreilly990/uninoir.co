Uni Noir

Welcome to Uni Noir

Darkness is not an entity but simply the absence of light, let us help you find the light within yourself.

I wanted to create a space where people felt safe and comfortable in order to connect and learn.

There was a time where I battled with depression and I told myself if I could get through this I would create an environment where I could teach people about the importance of mental health and give them the guidance and support they deserved.

Internal growth and self actualization is a topic people take for granted and it is ultimately what determines your success, I have been blessed with successful mentors who have educated and guided me to the level where I can educate other.

All events and workshops are aligned with my intentions and the companies values.

Respect, Equality, Freedom, Love, Oneness

Uni Noirs motto is to inform, inspire and impart.

My events consist of the following:

Raising money for charity

Supporting Communities

Creating Awareness

Educating

Mindfulness

Spreading love
